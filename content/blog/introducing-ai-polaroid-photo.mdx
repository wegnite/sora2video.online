# 🎬 Introducing Sora2 Video: Cinematic Automation for Every Launch

Sora2 Video is the orchestration layer for cinematic AI campaigns. Instead of juggling scattered prompt docs, annotation sheets, and delivery spreadsheets, Sora2 keeps everything inside one secure tool station. Draft sora prompts, manage review cycles, and publish mastered assets without leaving the console.

## ✨ Why Teams Standardize on Sora2 Video

### 🎞️ Connected Prompt-to-Scene Workflow
The Sora Prompt Lab structures every prompt with context, physics cues, and delivery tokens. Compare variants side by side, pin winning camera moves, and commit approved takes directly to the timeline.

### 🤝 Governance Built In
Role-based workspaces keep creative, marketing, legal, and localization in sync. Reviewers annotate frames in real time, approvals are logged automatically, and localization macros generate caption packs plus territory metadata in one click.

### 📊 Intelligence That Guides Every Iteration
Insight dashboards surface engagement forecasts, retention scores, and callouts where refinements matter. Benchmark upcoming launches against previous campaigns to prioritize boosts, media spend, and asset reuse.
